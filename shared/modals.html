  • Light Output

    How much light output do you need?

    35 Watt Ballast

    More efficient, longer lasting. Industry Standard in all vehicles that come factory-equipped with HID headlights. Best for use on vehicles with Canbus systems or weaker than average electrical systems (old vehicles, motorcycles, etc).

    Lumens: 5800-6200
    Lifespan: ~2,000 hrs
    Power Consumption: Average
    Color Shift: None

    55 Watt Ballast

    Brighter, more power hungry, lesser lifespan. Produces more output compared to all factory-installed HID headlight ballasts. Not recommended without the use of a relayed wire harness or in smaller projector and/or housings (fog lights).

    Lumens: 9100-9700
    Lifespan: ~1,500 hrs
    Power Consumption: Above Average
    Color Shift: Yes

  • Light Color

    What color is right for you?

    3000k (Golden Yellow)

    Recommended for fog lights only, the yellow light penetrates through particles in the air more effectively than white light does. Great for poor weather.
    ~2800 lumens (35w)

    4500k (Warm White)

    Vehicles with factory installed xenon headlights use 4300K. Best for all-season driving (mix of clear and poor weather).
    ~3400 lumens (35w)

    5500k (Pure White)

    Our most popular, though slightly less lumens, most people find the increase in contrast at from the whiter light improves overall vision.
    ~3300 lumens (35w)

    6500k (Blue Tinge)

    For those looking seeking a more stand-out look compared to stock, without over-compromising the amount of useful light output.
    ~3100 lumens (35w)
